Yahtzee Rules

  1. The game consists of 15 rounds, each round representing a scoring category.
  2. In each round, a player gets up to three opportunities to roll the dice.
  3. The player can choose to re-roll some or all dice on the second and third throw.
  4. After rolling the dice, the player scores based on the category of the current round and the dice values.
  5. Each category can only be scored once and the game ends when all categories have been scored.
  6. If a player scores a total of 63 points or more in the first six rounds (Ones through Sixes), they will receive a bonus of 35 points.
  7. The bonus is added to the player's total score.
  8. The player with the highest total score at the end of the game wins.

Good luck and have fun playing Yahtzee!
