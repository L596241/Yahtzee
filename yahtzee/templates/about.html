About

Welcome to my Yahtzee game! This game is the final project for my CS50 online course at Harvard. I've used a lot of what I learned in the course and also picked up some new skills along the way.

Making this game was a fun challenge. I had to figure out the game logic and make the interface easy to use. Every part of the project was a learning experience, and I enjoyed it alot.

I'm proud of how this game turned out. Whether you're here to play a round or just to see what it can do, I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed making it. Thank you!
